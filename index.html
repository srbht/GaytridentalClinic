Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about visits, emergencies, and treatments.

Do you treat dental emergencies and facial injuries?

Yes. We handle toothaches, swelling, knocked-out or broken teeth, and facial trauma such as cuts and jaw injuries. For urgent dental/maxillofacial attention, call our emergency line at 94151 21000. For life-threatening emergencies, please use local emergency services first.

Where are you located and what are your hours?

We're near Droliya Hospital, New Flyover, Gonda Road, Bahraich — 271801. We're open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM–8:00 PM and Sunday 10:00 AM–2:00 PM. Parking is available on site.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Both work. We recommend booking online or by phone to reduce waiting—same-week slots are usually available. Walk-ins are welcome and emergencies are prioritised.

What treatments do you offer?

General & cosmetic dentistry, oral surgery, dental implants, maxillofacial surgery, face trauma care, pediatric dentistry, orthodontics (braces & aligners), and root canal treatment—all under one roof.

Is root canal or extraction painful?

Procedures are done under local anaesthesia, so you stay comfortable. We explain each step, work at your pace, and give clear after-care instructions to manage any soreness.
